what momo does

🤍 one gentle check-in a day

at a random moment inside your awake hours — like a friend thinking of you, not an alarm. night-shift mode means he never wakes you post-call.

👂 a listening buddy

tell him about your day. he listens, reacts, and answers with warmth — never advice you didn't ask for, never "have you tried yoga?"

🫙 the memory jar

good moments get kept. on the hard days, momo brings one back: "you told me once… that happened. you did that."

🌪️ shake to vent

shake your phone, type the thing IN ALL CAPS, and momo chomps it to shreds. it is never saved. it is gone forever.

📱 he hangs out on your home screen

the widget rotates his poses and little encouragements all day — coffee in the morning, pajamas at night.

🌱 a streak that only counts up

days survived, never days "kept up." nothing to break, nothing to lose, no guilt. ever.

everything stays on your phone.
no account required. no cloud. no analytics. momo can't gossip — he doesn't even have wifi.
