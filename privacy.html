Privacy Policy

Momo for iOS · Last updated July 3, 2026

Momo is designed so that your private thoughts stay private. The short version: we do not collect, transmit, store, sell, or see any of your data. Ever.

What Momo stores, and where

  • Things you write to Momo, moments you save to the memory jar, your name, and your settings are stored only on your device, in the app's local database.
  • This data may be included in your normal encrypted iPhone backup (iCloud or computer), which is controlled by you and your Apple account — not by us.
  • Text you shred with shake-to-vent is never written to storage at all. When Momo shreds it, it is gone.

What Momo does NOT do

  • No accounts, no sign-in.
  • No servers. The app makes no network requests to us — we don't operate any backend.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • No data sharing with third parties, because there is no data to share.
  • No AI processing of what you write — Momo's responses come from a library written by humans, chosen on your device.

Notifications

Momo's daily check-ins are local notifications scheduled entirely on your device. They do not use a push server.

Health disclaimer

Momo is a comfort companion, not a medical or mental-health service, and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you're struggling, please reach out to someone qualified — in the US, you can call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).
