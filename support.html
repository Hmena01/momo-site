Common questions

Where is my data? On your phone, and only there. See the privacy policy.

Momo isn't sending check-ins? Check Settings → Notifications → Momo is allowed, and that the daily check-in toggle is on inside the app.

The widget isn't updating? Widgets refresh on Apple's schedule — give him up to an hour after adding. Opening the app nudges him along.
